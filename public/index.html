Why these numbers matter.

In 2025, residential values across Hamilton County rose roughly 59% in the countywide reappraisal. Tennessee's "certified tax rate" mechanism rolls millage down after a reappraisal so total county revenue stays flat — but the next big assessment-jump is locked in for 2029. If you buy today, your tax bill is calculated against the old appraisal until then.

Roughly translated: the gap between what you're paying now and what you'll pay in 2029 is real, knowable, and worth folding into your monthly budget — not a surprise to discover three years in.
